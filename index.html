About Me

I work as a researcher and engineer at SystemsResearch@Google (SRG), currently investigating frameworks for better security and privacy in agentic systems! In 2024, I graduated with my PhD at MIT in the PDOS group, where my thesis research aimed to design systems for better data protections and security in web applications. I am also broadly interested in multicore performance and scalability, and the application of formal methods in systems.

Besides research, I love playing violin, reading, hiking, climbing, and exploring the world around me. I am always free to sightread chamber music!
